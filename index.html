Insight

Insight is the Media Publishing Body of Indian Institute of Technology Bombay. It covers all the events and activities taking place inside the Institute such as life in IIT Bombay, Hostel life, Sports and Cultural Events and also Academics and Career related events in the Institute.


Insight also covers other things such as General Elections of the Institute and other currents issues related to India as well as the World. Insight Editors remain an independent third party while covering these events.


IIT BBC is the official broadcasting channel of Insight which tries to cover broadcasting of all the crucial events in the Institute.
